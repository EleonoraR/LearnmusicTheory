Lesson 1: Introduction to Scales

A scale is a series of notes arranged in ascending or descending order. Scales form the foundation for melodies and harmonies.

Major Scale

The major scale is the most commonly used scale in Western music. It consists of seven notes with a specific pattern of whole and half steps.

Pattern: Whole, Whole, Half, Whole, Whole, Whole, Half

Example: C Major Scale

Notes: C, D, E, F, G, A, B, C

Practice by playing the notes on your instrument or using an online piano keyboard.
